Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Faith & Fit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. We believe your spiritual and physical journey is deeply personal, which is why we built our app to process data locally whenever possible.

2. HealthKit Integration

We integrate with Apple HealthKit to read daily activity data (steps, active energy) and write workout data. This allows us to visualize your progress. We do not sell or transfer this data to third parties. All HealthKit data remains on your device or in your personal iCloud.

3. Data Collection

We do not collect personal data on our servers. Any profile information (name, goals, faith journey) is stored locally using on-device storage technologies (SwiftData).

4. Your Rights

Since data is stored locally, you have full control. You can delete your data at any time by deleting the app or interacting with the "Delete All Data" option in the settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about privacy, please contact us at support@faithandfit.app.
