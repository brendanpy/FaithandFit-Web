Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Faith & Fit ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Faith & Fit.

1. Information We Collect

Health Data (Apple HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, Faith & Fit integrates with Apple's HealthKit to:

  • Read your activity data (steps, workouts, calories burned) to visualize your progress.
  • Write workout data (calories, duration) when you complete a session in the app.

Important: Your health data is processed 100% locally on your device or in your personal iCloud. We do not sell, share, or transfer your HealthKit data to any third parties or advertising platforms.

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Profile information (Name, fitness goals, faith journey stage).
  • Workout preferences and constraints.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve the functionality of the app, including:

  • Personalizing your workout plans based on your fitness level.
  • Curating Bible verses relevant to your journey.
  • Tracking your consistency and progress.

3. Data Storage & Security

Faith & Fit is designed with privacy first. Your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device using SwiftData and UserDefaults.
  • Synced securely via your personal iCloud account (if enabled).

We do not maintain external servers that store your personal health data.
